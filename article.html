The Afterlife

Publication / Illustration

The Afterlife: An interactive exploration of Philippine Burial Archaeology is a book that dives into the rich, dense world of material culture in the ancient Filipino world. As a vessel of knowledge, this book serves as a tool to supplement history and social studies classes in the Philippine secondary education system. This fully-illustrated publication features articles about both famous and unfamiliar burial sites and practices from all over the Philippine islands. We collaborated with archaeologists from the University of the Philippines Archaeological Studies Program to develop the concept for this book. A second edition is currently being developed.
